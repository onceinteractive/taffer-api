PRIVACY POLICY

DEFINITIONS

  • The User – “you”, “your”
  • Taffer Media, Inc. – “we”, “us”, “our”
  • Taffer’s Bar HQ – “App”, “Application”

    • USER PROVIDED INFORMATION

  • The Application will collect and retain certain information for Registration and Log In processes. Personal information includes your full (first and last) name, password, email and team member status. Bar information includes bar name, bar address (including city and state) and type of venue. This information is required to use the Application.

    • INFORMATION AUTOMATICALLY COLLECTED

  • The Application will also automatically collect some information. This information includes access to posting for your personal social media pages as well as your Bar’s social media pages. The collection of this information is optional and you will be prompted to approve or decline providing said information when necessary. You may also opt out of automatically providing this information by uninstalling the Application.

    • TRACKING AND LOGGING DATA

  • Taffer Media, Inc. will store this information for as long as you use this Application and up to 60 months thereafter. You may request for your provided data to be completely deleted by emailing us at privacy@tafferbarhq.com.

    • SHARING OF INFORMATION

    Your information will be kept secure and private. Information will only be shared in one of three cases:
    1. If required by law;
    2. If disclosing said information is necessary to protect our rights, a person’s safety, investigate fraud, or respond to a government request;
    3. If Taffer Media, Inc. is involved in a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets. You will be notified in this case.

    CHILDREN AND THE UNDERAGE

  • Those under the age of twenty-one (21), or the legal drinking age in their place of residence, are not permitted to use this Application. This is also in compliance with the Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA). Those that do not meet age requirements will have their Application Profiles terminated.

    • SECURITY

  • Security of your information is our utmost priority. We will do everything in our power to safeguard and protect your provided information. Information will only be provided to authorized employees for whom it is absolutely necessary to maintain and run the Application. By agreeing to the Privacy Policy, you understand and acknowledge that no security system can prevent all potential security breaches.

    • CHANGES

  • This Privacy Policy may be updated at any time. When updated, you will be informed through the Application as well as in an email.

    • CONSENT

  • By installing the application, you agree to the Privacy Policy, current and future versions, as well as to providing the previously mentioned required information and our use(s) of said information.

    • CONTACT

  • If you have any questions about the Privacy Policy, our uses of your information, or other privacy practices while using the Application, please contact us at privacy@tafferbarhq.com.
